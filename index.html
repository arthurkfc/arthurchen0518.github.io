Arthur (Kefan) Chen

kefan_chen@brown.edu

I'm an incoming Ph.D. student in the Computer Science Department at Brown University. My advisor is Prof. Srinath Sridhar. My research interests are 3D computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning. I obtained my Bachelor Degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Toronto in 2018. After graduation, I had been working at Google Research for two years and I had the honor to collaborate with Dr. Ameesh Makadia and Prof. Noah Snavely. Currently, I'm working as a perception engineer at Gatik, an autonomous delivery startup.

During my undergrad study, I worked as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Sanja Fidler and I also worked on deep learning in animation and robotic perception as an intern at NVIDIA.
